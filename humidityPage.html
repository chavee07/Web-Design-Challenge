Humidity versus Latitude





It is normal to think that higher temperatures could positively contribute to humidity, however, as the graph above indicates, The Latitude vs. Humidity plot does not seem to have a strong correlation to latitude. While correlation between temperature and latitude suggested possible correlation with humidity, the weather data did not support this hypothesis. Although no correlation between Humidity v Latitude, it appears more cities have a humidity above 60% than a humidity below 60%. Grouping Northern vs. Southern Hemispheres could possibly reveal a trend for further analysis.
